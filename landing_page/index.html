The Readers' Platform

About BooktifuL

Readers are everywhere, so are books. As a reader, I came to realize that it is not easy to source books and obtain a readable copy. That's why I developed BooktifuL to make it a stop-shop for all books readers dream about.

I developed this project in a span of 2 weeks as my portfolio project to conclude the end of foundations curriculum of the Holberton School Software Engineering Program.
